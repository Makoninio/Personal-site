About Me

My name is Tanaka Makoni and I am a sophomore computer science student studying how humans and technology meet, with a growing interest in designing and developing tools that genuinely serve the people who use them. Through my human computer interaction coursework and personal projects, I’ve developed into a builder who cares deeply about clarity, aesthetics, and thoughtful design. I’m eager to keep learning about Design Thinking and to apply those principles in future software projects and create design that feels intuitive and convenient. My goal is to shape the technology I build from a human lens and create systems that feel supportive, empowering, and easy to use.
